🧭 About Yantrakart

Welcome to Yantrakart! 🚀 We are a student-led startup at Chandigarh University, committed to providing affordable and practical IoT, CAD, and software-based projects tailored for first-year engineering students. Recognizing that many students in their initial year lack hands-on experience and time to delve into complex projects, Yantrakart offers ready-to-use solutions that bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application.

💡 Our Offerings

  • 🔌 IoT Projects: Smart home automation, environmental monitoring, and more.
  • 🖥️ CAD Projects: SolidWorks, AutoCAD models, and mechanical design projects.
  • 💻 Software Projects: Desktop and web-based tools for engineering students.

🎯 Why Choose Yantrakart?

  • 💸 Affordable Pricing: Competitive prices for all students.
  • 🤝 Student-Centric Approach: Built by students, for students.
  • 📚 Comprehensive Support: We help you implement and understand your project.

How to Order 🛍️

  1. Login to your account to browse available projects.
  2. Select your desired items and click “Buy Now”.
  3. You will be redirected to the Checkout page to place your order.
  4. Our team will review your order after submission.
  5. If verified, you’ll receive a notification with a 50% payment link.
  6. Pay 50% to confirm your order. The rest is due at delivery.

Note: Please login to browse and order projects.

Delivery Process 🚚

To confirm your order, a 50% advance payment is required after order verification. The remaining 50% can be paid at the time of delivery.

Delivery is mainly done within Chandigarh University campus by our student team.

For delivery outside the campus, additional delivery charges may apply.

We strive to provide a smooth and hassle-free delivery experience for our fellow students! 🎓✨
